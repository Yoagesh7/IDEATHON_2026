SCREENING PROCESS

PHASES

A rigorous 3-day evaluation journey to identify and nurture the most impactful innovations

DAY 1

Preliminary Screening

Idea Relevance & Innovation

Objective

Filter ideas based on relevance and originality. Ensuring the core concept aligns with the competition goals.

Format

Short idea pitch / abstract review

Duration

7 min pitch + 3 min Q&A

Submission

Problem statement + proposed solution (1 page)

lightbulb

DAY 2

Intermediate Screening

Concept Strength & Feasibility

Objective

Evaluate depth and practicality of shortlisted ideas. Deep dive into the mechanics of the solution.

Format

Detailed concept presentation

Duration

10 min pitch + 5 min Q&A

Submission

Solution architecture, Implementation plan, Value proposition

Final Screening

Impact & Execution

Objective

Submission
